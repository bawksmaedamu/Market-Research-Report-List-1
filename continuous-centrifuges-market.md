<p><ul><li>Continuous Filtration Centrifuges</li><li>Continuous Sedimentation Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous centrifuges market comprises two main types: continuous filtration centrifuges and continuous sedimentation centrifuges. Continuous filtration centrifuges are designed to separate solids from liquids in a constant flow, efficiently processing large volumes with minimal downtime. In contrast, continuous sedimentation centrifuges utilize gravitational forces to settle solids from the liquid phase over extended periods, making them ideal for applications requiring prolonged separation. Both types are vital in industries like pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing for enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
