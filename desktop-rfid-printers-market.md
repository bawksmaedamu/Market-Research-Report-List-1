<p><strong>The Desktop RFID Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UHF</li><li>HF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desktop RFID printers come in two main types based on frequency: Ultra High Frequency (UHF) and High Frequency (HF). UHF printers operate in the 860-960 MHz range, enabling longer read distances and faster tag processing, making them ideal for inventory and supply chain applications. In contrast, HF printers work in the 13.56 MHz range, offering shorter read distances but better performance in environments with metal or liquids. They are commonly used for access control, payment systems, and asset tracking.</p></p>
