<p><p>The bio enzymes market consists of various types, each serving specific functions across industries. Pectinase is used mainly in fruit juice processing and clarification. Lipase aids in fat breakdown, essential in food and pharmaceuticals. Protease assists in protein digestion and is crucial in animal feed and detergents. Cellulase helps in biomass conversion, enhancing biofuel production. Catalase protects cells by decomposing hydrogen peroxide, while amylase converts starch into sugars, widely utilized in baking and brewing. Each enzyme plays a vital role in optimizing processes and improving efficiency.</p></p>
