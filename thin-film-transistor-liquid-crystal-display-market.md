<p><ul><li>TN</li><li>VA</li><li>IPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display (TFT-LCD) market is primarily categorized into three types: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN panels are known for their fast response times and lower production costs, making them suitable for gaming and budget displays. VA panels offer better contrast and deeper blacks, ideal for media consumption. IPS panels provide superior color accuracy and wider viewing angles, making them popular for professional work and high-end displays.</p></p>
