<p><p>Replaceable tip drills are categorized into several types based on their shank designs. Straight shank drills feature a consistent diameter along the shank, allowing for easy clamping and compatibility with standard chucks. Taper shank drills have a tapered end that provides a secure fit in the machine spindle, enhancing stability during use. Other types may include specialized designs like stepped or threaded shanks, catering to specific applications. Each type serves unique functions in various drilling environments, ensuring versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
