<p><p>The automobile seat belt market comprises different types designed to enhance passenger safety. Two-point seat belts, mainly lap belts, secure passengers at the hips. Three-point seat belts combine a lap and shoulder strap, offering better protection and are standard in most vehicles. Four-point and five-point seat belts are commonly found in racing cars and high-performance vehicles, providing additional stability. Six-point belts include extra straps to secure the shoulders and lap, ideal for motorsport applications. Other variations cater to specialized needs.</p></p>
