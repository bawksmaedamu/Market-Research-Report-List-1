<p><p>The couplant heater market can be categorized into two types: single cup and double cup heaters. Single cup couplant heaters are designed to warm a single container of couplant fluid, making them suitable for small-scale applications or individual use. In contrast, double cup couplant heaters accommodate two containers, facilitating simultaneous heating for larger operations or multiple users. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring optimal performance in non-destructive testing by maintaining the ideal temperature for effective coupling between surfaces.</p></p>
