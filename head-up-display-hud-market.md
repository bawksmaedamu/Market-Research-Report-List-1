<p><p>Head-up displays (HUD) are systems that project essential information onto a transparent surface, allowing users to maintain peripheral awareness. The market can be categorized into three types: C-HUD (Conventional HUD), which displays standard data like speed and navigation; W-HUD (Windshield HUD), integrating information directly on the vehicle's windshield for enhanced driver engagement; and AR-HUD (Augmented Reality HUD), which overlays digital information onto real-world views, offering immersive experiences that enhance situational awareness and safety.</p></p>
