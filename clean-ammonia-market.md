<p><p>The clean ammonia market comprises primarily two types: blue ammonia and green ammonia. Blue ammonia is produced from natural gas through carbon capture technology, which mitigates greenhouse gas emissions while utilizing existing infrastructure. In contrast, green ammonia is synthesized using renewable energy sources, such as wind or solar, combined with water to produce hydrogen, ensuring a fully sustainable production process. Both types aim to facilitate the transition to low-carbon energy systems, supporting decarbonization efforts across various industries.</p></p>
