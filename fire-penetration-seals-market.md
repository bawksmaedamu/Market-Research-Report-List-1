<p><p>The fire penetration seals market includes various types designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke through openings in walls, floors, and ceilings. Foam seals are flexible and expand when exposed to heat, effectively sealing gaps. Block seals consist of solid materials that fill openings and provide a robust barrier. Ablative coatings are applied to surfaces and gradually erode when exposed to high temperatures, protecting the underlying material. Other types may include hybrid systems or innovative materials that enhance fire safety.</p></p>
