<p><p>The Multi-Mode Fiber Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) market consists of two primary configurations: Single-end and Loop. In a Single-end Configuration, temperature measurements are taken from one end of the fiber, allowing for simpler installation and effective monitoring over shorter distances. Loop Configuration, on the other hand, involves a fiber loop that enables continuous temperature sensing along the entire length, optimizing measurement accuracy and coverage. Both configurations are crucial for various applications, including industrial monitoring and infrastructure safety.</p></p>
