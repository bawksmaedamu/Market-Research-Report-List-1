<p><ul><li>Pipe End Type</li><li>Flange Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Expansion Joint market is characterized by two primary types: Pipe End Type and Flange Type. Pipe End Type expansion joints are directly attached to the ends of pipes, providing flexibility and accommodating thermal expansion or movement. Flange Type expansion joints feature flanged connections, allowing for easier installation and maintenance between connected pipes. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, ensuring the integrity of piping systems by absorbing vibrations and minimizing stress, thus enhancing overall system performance and longevity.</p></p>
