<p><p>Horizontal hydraulic pipe benders are categorized into three main types based on their operation and automation level. Manual benders require operators to exert physical force, providing simplicity and control for small projects. Semi-automatic benders combine manual input with automated features, enhancing efficiency while still requiring some operator intervention. Fully automatic benders are designed for high-volume production, utilizing advanced technology and precise programming to bend pipes with minimal human input, ensuring consistent quality and speed in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
