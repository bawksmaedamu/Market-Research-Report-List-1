<p><p>The carbon fiber drive shaft market includes two main types: rigid shafts and hollow shafts. Rigid shafts are solid and provide excellent strength and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications that require minimal flex. Conversely, hollow shafts are lighter and offer improved torque transmission while reducing weight, enhancing fuel efficiency. Both types leverage the lightweight and high-strength properties of carbon fiber, catering to diverse automotive and industrial applications, from racing vehicles to machinery, where performance and efficiency are critical.</p></p>
