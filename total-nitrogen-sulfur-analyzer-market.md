<p><ul><li>Carried Out by the CLD Method</li><li>Carried Out by the Dumas Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Total Nitrogen and Sulfur Analyzer market is segmented based on the analytical methods employed. The CLD (Chemiluminescence Detector) method measures nitrogen compounds by detecting emitted light during a chemical reaction, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, the Dumas method involves combustion of the sample in a nitrogen-rich environment, allowing direct measurement of nitrogen and sulfur content via gas chromatography. Both techniques cater to various industries, including environmental monitoring, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring precise elemental analysis.</p></p>
