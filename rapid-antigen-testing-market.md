<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rapid Antigen Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunosorbent Assay</li><li>Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent</li><li>Precipitation Reaction</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Agglutination Reaction</li><li>Complement Fixatio</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Antigen Testing market encompasses various types of immunological assays. Immunosorbent assays utilize solid-phase binding to detect antigens. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) use enzymes for signal amplification. Precipitation reactions measure antigen-antibody complexes by forming visible aggregates. Radioimmunoassays rely on radioactive labels for sensitivity. Agglutination reactions involve the clumping of particles in response to antigens, while complement fixation tests assess immune reactions through cell lysis. Each method offers unique advantages for rapid detection of pathogens.</p></p>
