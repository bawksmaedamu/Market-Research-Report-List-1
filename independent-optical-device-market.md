<p><ul><li>Active Device</li><li>Passive Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The independent optical device market comprises two primary segments: active devices and passive devices. Active devices generate and manipulate light, such as lasers and photodiodes, playing crucial roles in communication and sensing applications. In contrast, passive devices do not generate light but instead rely on external light sources to function; examples include optical filters and beam splitters. Together, these devices enhance optical systems, enabling advancements in telecommunications, information technology, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
