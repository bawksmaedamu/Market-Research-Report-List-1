<p><p>The aluminium alloys market is divided into two primary types: deformed aluminium alloys and cast aluminium alloys. Deformed aluminium alloys, commonly used in construction and automotive industries, are produced through processes like rolling and extruding, resulting in strong, lightweight materials. In contrast, cast aluminium alloys are formed by pouring molten aluminium into molds, allowing for complex shapes and sizes; they are widely used in aerospace, electronics, and manufacturing sectors due to their versatility and excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
