<p><p>Refrigerated benchtop centrifuges are essential laboratory instruments used for separating substances based on density. The market is categorized into low-speed and high-speed centrifuges. High-speed centrifuges, operating above 10,000 RPM, are used for more critical procedures, like isolating DNA, RNA, or proteins, where higher centrifugal forces are necessary for efficient separation and pelleting of samples.</p></p>
