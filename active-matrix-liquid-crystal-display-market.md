<p><p>The Active-matrix Liquid-crystal Display (AMLCD) market primarily consists of two types: Polycrystalline Silicon (poly-Si) and Amorphous Silicon (a-Si). Poly-Si technology offers higher resolution, faster response times, and greater efficiency, making it ideal for premium displays, such as in TVs and high-end monitors. In contrast, a-Si is more cost-effective and widely used for lower-end displays, like those in laptops and mobile devices. Both types cater to various consumer demands, influencing overall display technology advancements.</p></p>
