<p><strong>The 5G Smart Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switched Multi-beam Antenna</li><li>Adaptive Array Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G Smart Antenna market features two prominent types: Switched Multi-beam Antennas and Adaptive Array Antennas. Switched Multi-beam Antennas utilize multiple fixed beams to cover various sectors, enhancing network coverage and capacity by switching between beams based on demand. In contrast, Adaptive Array Antennas dynamically adjust beam patterns and directions in real-time, optimizing signal quality and user experience. Both technologies enable efficient resource allocation, reduce interference, and improve overall network performance, crucial for the high-speed and low-latency requirements of 5G.</p></p>
