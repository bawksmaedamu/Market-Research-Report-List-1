<p><p>The EM brake market comprises various types catering to diverse applications. Single face brakes offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while power-off brakes engage when power is cut, providing safety. Particle brakes utilize magnetic particles for smooth braking control, and hysteresis power brakes offer precision through magnetic fields, minimizing wear. Multiple disk brakes incorporate multiple friction surfaces for enhanced braking force and efficiency. Each type serves specific requirements in industrial, automotive, and aerospace sectors, ensuring reliable performance and safety.</p></p>
