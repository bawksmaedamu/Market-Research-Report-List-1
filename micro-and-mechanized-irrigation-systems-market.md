<p><p>Micro and mechanized irrigation systems enhance agricultural efficiency by optimizing water use. The sprinkler system distributes water through overhead pipes, ideal for various crops. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots, minimizing waste and promoting growth in water-scarce areas. Central pivot systems rotate around a fixed point, irrigating large fields uniformly, suitable for expansive farms. Lateral move systems operate on wheels, moving laterally across fields for even water distribution, offering flexibility and efficiency in crop management.</p></p>
